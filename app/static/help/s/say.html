Help on: say

Syntax:say <something>
Level:any
Minimum:say or '

Say is one of the basic communication commands. When you say something, it goes to everyone in the room. If the speaker is invisible to the listener, the listener gets "Someone" as the identity of the speaker.

See also: Address, Whisper, Socials, Emote, Communication, Invisibility, Rooms
