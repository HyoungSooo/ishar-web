Help on: spell cripple

Syntax:cast cripple <victim>
Class:Cleric
Level:7
Save:against petrification for no effect

A person who is crippled is unable to stand and suffers a small strength penalty. These effects lasts for a few mud hours. An opponent who cannot stand is unable to move, cast spells, or flee from combat. The effects of the cripple spell can be neutralized by application of the Remove Paralysis and Heal spells.

See also: Spell Remove Paralysis, Spell Heal
