Help on: spell teleport

Syntax:cast teleport
Class:Magician
Level:10
Save:none

Teleportation allows the well-versed mage to instantaneously transport body and possessions to a new location by following one of many pathways visible only to the mage. These pathways require no mana to traverse, but neither can they be controlled, and some pathways may end in death for the mage. The act of following a pathway, or even attempting to follow one, disrupts the mage's perception of pathways for a short period of time, so teleportation can only be performed periodically.

See also: Spell Blink
