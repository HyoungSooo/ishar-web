Help on: spell bone shards

Syntax:cast 'bone shards' <victim>
Class:Necromancer
Level:4
Save:against magic for reduced damage and paralysis for no weakening

Necromancers casting this spell fire a volley of sharp bone shards at an opponent, dealing light damage and temporarily weakening the victim's armor.

NOTE: Applications do not currently stack.

See also: Spell Drain, Spell Skeletal Grasp, Spell Blight, Spell Reave
