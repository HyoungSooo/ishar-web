Help on: spell summon corpse

Syntax:cast 'summon corpse' <corpse>
Class:Necromancer
Level:12 (16) (23)
Save:none

Necromancers of level 12 and above can use this spell to summon a corpse to their location. At level 16, they gain the ability to summon the corpses of other players. The spell fails if either the caster or the corpse are in a location that prevents summoning. They may over come this limitation at level 20 with full Spell Potency.

See also: Spell Summon, Spell Potency
