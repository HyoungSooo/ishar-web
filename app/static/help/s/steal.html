Help on: steal

Syntax:steal <thing> <person>
  steal <thing> <container> <person>
Class:Rogue (others can use against sleeping victims)
Level:3
Minimum:steal

A rogue can steal coins or objects from other players and from mobiles. Be careful though, you might get caught and most mobiles and players take a dim view of this activity.

You can also steal things from inside other people's containers, with the same results if you are caught trying. You may have to open those containers first, and may want to close them afterwards, and you will almost certainly want to look inside before trying to take something.

See also: Rogue, Watch
