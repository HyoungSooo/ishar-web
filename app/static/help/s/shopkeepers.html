Help on: shopkeepers

There is a certain class of mobile known collectively as 'shopkeepers.' A shopkeeper's primary function is to provide objects to players at a price. These objects include food, clothing, weapons, etc., and the shopkeepers charge varying amounts of gold for the objects they sell. When you trade with a shopkeeper, the number of coins are automatically added to or subtracted from your purse. The coins MUST be in your purse, not in other containers.

Shopkeeper commands: Buy, Sell, Value, List
