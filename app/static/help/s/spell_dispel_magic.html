Help on: spell dispel magic

Syntax:cast 'dispel magic' <target>
Class:Magician
Level:6
Save:none

When cast on an invisible object, the Dispel Magic spell causes it to become visible. This change is permanent unless another invisibility spell is cast upon the object.

When cast on a person, the Dispel Magic spell can nullify the following spells: Charm, Invisible, Second Sight, Silence.

See also: Spell Remove Curse, Spell Detect Magic
