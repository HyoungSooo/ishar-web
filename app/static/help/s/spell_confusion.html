Help on: spell confusion

Syntax:cast confusion <victim>
Class:Magician
Level:9
Save:against magic for no effect

Confusion causes the victim to temporarily lose his/her bearings, possibly travelling in directions other than they intend when moving. Additionally, the victim's combat is slower and less accurate. The duration is fairly short, lasting roughly 1 to 4 mud hours depending upon the level of the caster. A confused person will suffer a penalty to their Focus proportional to the level of the spell and a decrease in speed of 10%.

See also: Focus
