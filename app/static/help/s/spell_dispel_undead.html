Help on: spell dispel undead

Syntax:cast 'dispel undead' <victim>
Class:Cleric
Level:7
Save:none, but may fail if the victim is close to the caster's level

This spell turns an Undead creature to dust instantly if it is not specially protected by a deity and is close to or less than the level of the caster.

If the creature is not Undead, but is of evil alignment and the caster is of good alignment, the <victim> suffers an amount of damage between <level of caster> and 2*<level of caster>.

See also: Alignment, Spell Sanctuary
