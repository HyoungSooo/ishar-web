Help on: spell corpse walk

Syntax:cast 'corpse walk' <corpse>
Class:Necromancer
Level:4
Save:none

This spell forces a corpse to its feet and causes it to follow the caster. The corpse cannot be ordered to perform any tasks, but it will continue to follow the caster until it is "killed" again, effectively ending the spell. The walking corpse is not harmed by the effects of cold climates or drowning, but heat and long falls will kill it, as will any strikes it takes from players or creatures. The primary purpose of corpse walk is to allow necromancers to return the fallen corpses of their comrades to town with equipment intact.

Unfortunately, corpse walk will not work on player corpses for now.
