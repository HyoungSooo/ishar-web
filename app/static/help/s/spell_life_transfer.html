Help on: spell life transfer

Syntax:cast 'life transfer' <target 1> <target 2>
Class:Necromancer
Level:9
Save:none

Through the use of this spell, a necromancer can shift life from one groupmate to another. Either the recipient or the benefactor may be the caster. <Target 1> loses a quarter of their life force, and <target 2> gains an amount equal to the amount lost by <target 1>.
