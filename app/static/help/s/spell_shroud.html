Help on: spell shroud

Syntax:cast shroud <target>
Class:Necromancer
Level:2
Save:none

A necromancer has the ability to wrap people with a protective shroud. The shroud increases <target>'s armor and envelops them with darkness. A person may not both be protected by a shroud and sanctuary at the same time. Shroud lasts for a long duration.

See also: Spell Sanctuary
