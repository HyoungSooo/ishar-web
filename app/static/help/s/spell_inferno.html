Help on: spell inferno

Syntax:cast inferno <victim>
Class:Magician
Level:16
Save:against magic for reduced damage

Magicians of level 16 or higher can use this spell to engulf their foes in a huge torrent of fire, causing massive damage. <Target> takes the brunt of the damage and all other opponents of the group may possibly take splash damage.
