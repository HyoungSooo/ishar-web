Help on: spell sleep

Syntax:cast sleep <victim>
Class:Magician
Level:10
Save:against magic for no effect

A victim who fails his saving roll is forced to sleep for up to 12 hours. The victim can only be awakened by being harmed or application of Dispel Magic. Victims of this spell are not happy with the mage, for obvious reasons, so watch out.

A person not affected by the spell may awaken the sleeper.

See also: Spell Dispel Magic, Wake
