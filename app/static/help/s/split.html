Help on: split

Syntax:split <amount> <type>
Level:any
Minimum:sp

The split command is used by a group leader to split a number of coins between his/her followers. Example: split 30 obsidian. The leader automatically keeps coins remaining from a split, and is included in the split, i.e., if you split 20 obsidian coins between a group of 4, you see:

You give 5 obsidian coins to Zrat. You give 5 obsidian coins to Sandstorm. You give 5 obsidian coins to Jeem.

and the other 5 are automatically kept by you.
