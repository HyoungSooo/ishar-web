Help on: spell retribution

Syntax:cast retribution
Class:Cleric
Level:16
Save:against magic for no effect

A cleric who resides in the favor of their chosen deity may invoke that deity's retribution against their foes for a short while. When struck in combat, the deity may return a small amount of damage back on the attacker in divine wrath.

The damage is calculated as a range between 1 - 50% of the damage received. I.e. If you take a 50 damage hit, you'll roll a 1d25 to hit back. This damage may not be lethal, same as harm.

See also: Divine Purpose
