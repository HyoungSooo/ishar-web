Help on: spell reave

Syntax:cast reave <victim>
Class:Necromancer
Level:13
Save:against magic for reduced damage

The use of this spell viciously reaves the soul of <victim>, causing very heavy damage and lightly healing the caster and all groupmates of the caster.

See also: Spell Drain, Spell Bone Shards, Spell Skeletal Grasp, Spell Blight
