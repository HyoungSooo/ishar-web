Help on: spell mist

Syntax:cast mist
Class:Cleric
Level:17
Save:none

Mist enables a cleric to transform the air around him or her into a vaporous mist, which has a beneficial healing and invigorating affect on all creatures present for 6 mud hours. The most powerful clerics are capable of producing a most effective healing mist.

Spell Potency Bonuses:

Clerics who opt for Spell Potency will find their mists to be even more effective. At lower tiers, their mists will be able to rejuvenate the spirit as well as the flesh. At the highest power, their mists will be able to differentiate friend from foe.
