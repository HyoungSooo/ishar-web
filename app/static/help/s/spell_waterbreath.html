Help on: spell waterbreath

Syntax:cast 'waterbreath' <target>
Level:3
Class:Magician
Save:none

This spell gives <target> the ability to breathe under water for 12 mud hours. When it runs out, the <target> starts to drown if still underwater.

See also: Spell Endure Heat, Spell Endure Cold, Spell Feather Fall
