Help on: spell enchant

Syntax:cast enchant <object>
Class:Magician
Level:11
Save:none

This spell improves the efficacy of some non-magical weapons and armor. Some have heard an enchant to have some unpredictable effects on various legendary items.

On non-magical, items attack, damage and armor bonuses may increase with the level of the spell. Beware, however the object may be destroyed by this process if it is forbidden to someone of the enchanter's alignment, or if it is already enchanted.

Some magicians around the world will enchant your items for you, for a price.

Spell Potency Bonuses:

Spell Potency allows you to enchant you gear with greater power, granting new abilities to weapons and armor. Weapons may gain elemental power and armor will be more resistant to damage from acid.

See also: Spell Bless, Spell Legend Lore, Spell Possession, Spell Potency
