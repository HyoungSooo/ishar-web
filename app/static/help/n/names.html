Help on: names

    Names are expected to remotely fit into a medieval fantasy world.  Avoid
well-known characters from books or movies; be original.  Avoid your real
name.  One word names, please.  Don't mash words together.  No vulgarity will
be tolerated.

Offensive or vulgar names will be deleted without warning, possibly after you've put a lot of time into the character. To avoid this, take the time to choose something reasonable.
