Help on: necromancer

Many hours of study deep within a crypt gives the Necromancer a sickly and pallid appearance. Though gaunt in form and physically weak, his ability to enhance his body through the dark magics and extensive research into organic tissues and pressure points give him a formidable melee advantage. Coupled with an arsenal of life draining spells and the signature undead army, the Necromancer is what nightmares are made of.
