Help on: parry

Syntax:parry
Class:Warrior
Level:3
Minimum:par
Stats:Agility/Focus

While in combat, a warrior can go on the defensive and concentrate on parrying attacks by his opponent. You will give up some damage as a result, but if your opponent is a poor fighter, you may parry and stop an attack altogether. Parrying requires that you be wielding a weapon.
