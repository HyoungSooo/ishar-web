Help on: protect

Syntax:protect [<person>]
Level:any
Minimum:prot

Protect someone from harm. When protecting someone, you will intercept attacks made against that person provided that you are free to do so and can see both the attacker and the victim. You may miss backstab attacks unless you are also watching the rogue.

You cannot defend others against magical attacks.

See also: Watch, Attack, Backstab, Bash, Kick
