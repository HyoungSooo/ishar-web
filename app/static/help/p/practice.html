Help on: practice

Syntax:practice [<skill>]
Level:any
Minimum:pra

Practice, with no arguments, reports your skills, proficiency, and number of unused practices.

If you have unused practices and are in the presence of a guild master for your guild, you can possibly improve your skills by practicing them. A guildmaster tells you if you can train with him/her or not, and if you can, they tell you which skills and spells they teach, along with how many practice credits and possibly how many coins they charge. There are many guildmasters for every class; as you reach higher levels you must seek them out.
