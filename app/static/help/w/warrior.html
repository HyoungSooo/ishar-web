Help on: warrior

Conditioned since childhood, physical prowess and martial skills are the Warrior's advantage. His strength and durability allow him to survive extreme physical trauma, while his combat skills give him the ability to wreck havoc among his enemies. 

   Skills: Shield Block, Bash, Kick, Parry, Rescue, Disarm, Hunt, Parry,
           Cleave, Enrage, Riposte, Taunt, Die Hard, Critical Specialization,
		   Rallying Cry
