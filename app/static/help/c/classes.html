Help on: classes

When a person creates a character, one of the most important choices he or she makes is which class the character will be. There are five possible choices: Warrior, Rogue, Magician, Necromancer and Cleric. Each has its own set of skills and requires a somewhat different style of play. For many adventures the combined skills of all classes are required for survival. Each class has its own guild and guildmaster and to advance your standing you must train with the guildmaster for your class.

Available Classes: Warrior, Rogue, Magician, Cleric, Necromancer
