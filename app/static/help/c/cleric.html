Help on: cleric

A life devoted to Deity is the root of the Cleric's power. His magic is of divine origin, not requiring the unhealthful years of physically debilitating study, so the Cleric is more physically powerful than the Magician. Since Clerical magic is the earthly manifestation of the power of God, it gives the Cleric the power to heal as well as destroy, to extend the warm comfort of a loving God or to lash out and lay waste to that which is unclean.
