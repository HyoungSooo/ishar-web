Help on: communication points

To prevent people from being spammy to everyone in the game, points are used for some forms of communication. They are regained slowly over approximately 3 hours.

Say, address, whisper, gtell, socials, and yell are never limited and never take any communication points. Tell, ask, and reply are always free to people in your group, people in the same room as you, and people who have used tell, ask, or reply to you in the last mud day.

All other forms of communication take points per person that sees your message. Repeatedly telling to someone who hasn't responded to you recently will quickly take away your voice. And shout will be most expensive as most mortals can hear you.

The first few hours that you are in the game with a new character, you might run into the limits. You hopefully won't normally.
