Help on: critical attack

Stats : Strength, Focus

Critical attacks can be turning points for the battle, whether it be a well-aimed strike or an unfocused swing for minimum damage.

A 'Well Aimed' hit is considered a critical hit, and deals out more than the maximum damage capable by your weapon, i.e., based on stats up to 150% your maximum damage. Items that affect critical affect your chance to land a critical attack. In addition, two-handed weapons have a greater chance of dealing a critical hit.

On the flip side of the coin, low strength and focus or negative bonuses can result in very 'Weak' hits.
