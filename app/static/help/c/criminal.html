Help on: criminal

Criminal status can be seen in how some mobs react to you. In particular, some will shout that there's a criminal and attack you on the spot.

Becoming a criminal is easy! PKing is a direct route. Having terrible karma is a slower way to being a criminal.

Criminal status can be removed by being killed by the elite guards Talmar or Madraelthon. Bad karma cannot.
