Help on: drop

Syntax:drop <object name>
Level:any
Minimum:drop

Drops an object from your inventory to the room you are standing in. The object must be in inventory to be dropped; if you are currently wearing it, you must "remove" it first. "Drop all" can be used to drop everything in your inventory, and "drop coins" drops all of the coins in your purse.

See also: Get, Take, Give, Put
