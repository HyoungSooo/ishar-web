Help on: die hard

Syntax:none
Class:Warrior
Level:15
Stats:Endurance/Willpower
    Battle-hardened and experienced, a powerful warrior may refuse to die when
struck a mortal blow.
    Once every Isharian day, when struck by an otherwise fatal blow, a warrior
will instead heal to 10% of their total hitpoints if they succeed a check
against their endurance and willpower. A sufficiently powerful hit, that reduces 
them further then 10% of their total hit points, they will be unable to
overcome the damage.
