Help on: down

Syntax:north, south, east, west, up, down, ne, nw, se, sw
Level:any
Minimum:n, s, e, w, u, d, ne, nw, se, sw

These commands move you to a room in the indicated direction, provided that the appropriate exit exists. They are equivalent to "go <exit name>" for exits named north, south, east, west, up or down. An exit could be too small for you to get through or there could be a closed door preventing passage.

NE, NW, SE, and SW are pseudo-commands used to move in the directions northeast, northwest, southeast, and southwest.

If you are mounted, your mount will carry you in that direction.

See also: Movement, Rooms, Go, Exits, Walking
