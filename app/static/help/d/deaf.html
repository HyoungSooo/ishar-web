Help on: deaf

This is rather self explanatory. If in the 'who' list you see {deaf} next to a player's name, it means they are sleeping, in a soundproof room, or a number of other reasons. Shouting, telling nor yelling will reach them. Your only hope is if you are Grouped with them, and can use Gtell.

See also: Who, Grouping, Gtell, Yell
