Help on: death

Stats : Endurance, Willpower

At 0 hitpoints, a player becomes stunned. Below that a player may be considered mortally wounded, and will slowly lose health until eventually they die. The healthiest of warriors are known to survive far into the negatives, while a groupmate runs to find help.

Creatures die at 0 hitpoints and have no stunned or wounded state. 

    When you die, you will lose experience and your items will stay with
your corpse where you died.  However, you may avoid death altogether by
sacrificing corpses to obtain Divine Favor.
