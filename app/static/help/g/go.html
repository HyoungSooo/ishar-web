Help on: go

Syntax:go <exit name>
Level:any
Minimum:go

This command moves you through the indicated exit if it is open and is large enough to permit your passage. The vast majority of exits are named north, south, east, west, up or down and can therefore be accessed through the use of n, s, e, w, u or d. There are times though when an exit might have a name such as "into the well." The go command is used to access such exits.

See also: Movement, North, South, East, West, Up, Down, Rooms, Exits
