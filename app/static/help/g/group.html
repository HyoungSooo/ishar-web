Help on: group

Syntax:group [<person>]
Level:any
Minimum:group

Used with no arguments, this command displays the members of a person's group. If <person> is used, this command alternately enrolls/expels that person from the group.

Only the group leader can enroll/expel group members. This is the person who everyone else is following (see `help follow').

Groups can only be formed of people who all have their Safety toggle on or all have Safety off. The group leader will get an error if a person he is trying to enroll has Safety set differently.

See also: Disband, Follow, Gtell, Glance, Safety, Yank, Level Restrictions
