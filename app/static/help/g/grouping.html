Help on: grouping

The primary function of the group for MUDders is so a team of players can share earned experience points fairly among the members of the group. Many adventures require the combined efforts of several character classes to be successfully completed.

The mechanics of grouping are: 1. Select one person to be the group's leader. 2. All other members FOLLOW <leader>. (See `help follow') 3. The leader enrolls followers with GROUP <follower>. (See `help group') 4. You should be within 3 levels of each other.

That's all there is to it. Now that you are all grouped, you can use the group tell command, (See `help gtell'), to talk amongst yourselves.
