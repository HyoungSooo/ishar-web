Help on: gods

Beyond mortality is a network of Immortals conspiring to create and alter the world in which you live. Immortals spend their time 'learning the ropes' of a life without fear of death. Often times Immortals find godly discipline too strict and are subsequently cast from grace. Artisans are Immortals who have accepted the ways of the omnipotent as well as proven to be competent artificers of order from formless chaos. Artisans dedicate themselves to adding exciting new areas to Ishar. Enduring countless eons as lesser Immortals and contributing substantially to the world you see around you are the hallmarks of the timeless Eternal, tireless Forger and -- of course -- the Gods.
