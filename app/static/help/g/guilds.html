Help on: guilds

In order to make use of a skill, you must first practice it. This is most often done in a guild, several of which are found in the town of Mareljda. Others exist in other cities, and there are individuals in the lands who have mastered certain unique skills and teach them as well.

While most guilds are open to all players of a specific class, there are special ones which teach only to certain alignments, races, players who have solved quests, or possess special items.

When you find your guildmaster, type "practice" to get a list of what skills they may be able to teach you.
