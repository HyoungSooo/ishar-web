Help on: health

Syntax:score
Level:any
Minimum:sc

Reports various information about your character. Includes health, spell energy, movement points, level, amount of gold, how much you are carrying, etc. Also supplies general character information such as time spent on the system. Any of `score', `status', `report', `health' can be used to check all of this info.

To find out how close you are to gaining a level, use train.

See also: Equipment, Inventory, Train
