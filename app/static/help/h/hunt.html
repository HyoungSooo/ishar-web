Help on: hunt

Syntax:hunt [<victim>]
Class:Warrior
Level:3
Minimum:hunt
Skills:Endurance/Perception

A warrior who is trying to find a person or mobile can use the hunt ability to track quarry. Hunt <victim> sets you off on the trail. Hunt with no arguments halts a hunt. Hunt does not find quarry through intervening closed doors. Neither can you hunt into a death trap, but you may find yourself in other, less immediately lethal hazards.
