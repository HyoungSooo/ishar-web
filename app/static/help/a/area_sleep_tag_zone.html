Ever jealous of the mythological Olympians, the gods of Ishar set out one day to create their own looking glass into a realm of mortal combat. The result was this strange region in which each room is formed of a pure element: earth, air, fire, or water. No harm can befall a mortal in this odd land, but special staves allow mortals to cast each other back to the normal world. Mortals have taken this as a game, and sometimes travel in small groups to the circle of stones just north of the Eastern Road in order to test their speed, accuracy, and luck against one another, all to the vast amusement of the watching gods. Rumor has it that a set of directions is posted in the entryway.
