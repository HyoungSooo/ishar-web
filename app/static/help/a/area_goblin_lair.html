Help on: area goblin lair

A small tribe of goblins have captured Princess Tabitha. Rumor has it that they will soon sacrifice her to the Dark Slayer, an evil god of death and destruction. Only you can stop these malicious malformed misanthropes.

The lair is to the northwest of town, high in the mountains near the source of the River Telemacus.

NOTES: Recommended Levels: 5-10 Group Size: 2-4
