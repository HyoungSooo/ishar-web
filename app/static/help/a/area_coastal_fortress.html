Help on: area coastal fortress

Long ago, when Mareldja was but a young city and the forces of evil yet played at her gates, the rulers of the city maintained a fine naval fleet and a troop of elite sailors garrisoned in a fortress on the coast.

After many years of peace, this force became superfluous and was disbanded; the fortress was allowed to remain empty, and the years have taken their toll. The ruin is now overrun with lizards, spiders, and vermin, which can pose an interesting challenge to the novice adventurer.

NOTES: Recommended Levels: 1-8 Group Size: 1-4
