Help on: area wolfnacht carnival

Blowing into the area over night, the Wolfnacht Carnival boasts a wide variety of entertainment not the least of which is the legendary arena. Though usually as quick to disappear as they are to set up a dozen massive tents, this carnival shows no signs of going anywhere. Keep an eye out for new booths and games as the Wolfnacht is ever expanding.

NOTES: Recommended Levels: 3 or higher for the arena Group Size: 2 or more needed to battle

The arena is back!

The carnival is located through Mareldja's Gate of Water and north along the coast road. Perched at the top of the first hill, it's difficult to miss.
