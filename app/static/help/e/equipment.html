Help on: equipment

Syntax:equipment
Level:any
Minimum:eq

The equipment command displays the items you are currently wearing. It does not show items you are carrying or items inside containers you are carrying. Items that are invisible to the naked eye will be surrounded by parentheses and visible to you whether or not you can see invisible.

See also: Inventory, Look, PK
