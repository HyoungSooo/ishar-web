Help on: emote

Syntax:emote <something>
Level:any
Minimum:em or ,

The emote command is used to add variety to conversations. What the command basically does is replace the 'emote' with your name on everyone else's screen. For example, if your name is Smurf and you execute: 

      emote LOVES Ishar!

everyone in the room with you sees: 

      Smurf LOVES Ishar!
