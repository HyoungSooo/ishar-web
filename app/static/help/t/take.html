Help on: take

Syntax:get <obj> [from] [<place>]
  take <obj> [from] [<place>]
Level:any
Minimum:get

For getting objects. A user can "get flower" to get a flower that is laying in the room or "get flower from box" if the flower is in a box. The word "all" can be substituted for <obj> to get everything in the room or to empty the box.

Objects often weigh something, and as such you cannot pick everything up. It is a good idea to keep an eye on your encumbrance.

See also: Objects, Put, Give, Drop, Encumbrance
