Help on: tell

Syntax:tell <person> <something>
Level:any
Minimum:t

Unlike the Say command, tell is a private method of communication. The <person> you specify is the only one who receives your words. If you are not visible to the recipient of the tell, you are identified as "Someone."

See also: Ask, Reply, Quiet, Ignore, Communication, Communication Points
