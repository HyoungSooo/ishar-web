Help on: taunt

Syntax:taunt
Class:Warrior
Level:11
Minimum:tau
Stats:Strength/Willpower
    A skilled and powerful warrior may let loose a challenge to all opponents
in the area, taunting them to attack him or her. Each foe may attempt to save
against distraction, but on a failed save they are forced to attack the warrior
regardless of their current foe and are enraged, taking a scaling penalty to
attack.
