Help on: trip

Syntax:trip [<opponent>]
Class:Rogue
Level:16
Minimum:tri
Stats:Agility/Perception

Trip allows a rogue to knock the opponent off balance and topple them to the ground, even if the rogue themselves is seated. Until regaining its feet, this can prevent the victim from fleeing or using spells, breath attacks, or combat skills. Victims also take additional damage when down.

See also: Rogue, Combat
