Help on: title

Syntax:title [<string>]
Level:1
Minimum:tit

This command allows you to set your title to whatever strikes your fancy. The title shows up on the who list and a few other places. If the string doesn't include your name in it, it will be prepended to the front. Without an argument, the command resets your title to the default for your level.

Don't put anything offensive in your title. Do not include the names of other players in your titles or obscure your name in any other way.

If your title is currently the default for your level, you can freely change it. Otherwise, changing your title uses communication points similarly to writing on the board.
