Help on: train

Syntax:train [<stat> [into] <stat>]
Level:any
Minimum:train

Train will show you your permanent statistics, how much experience you have to level, and various auxillary statistics.

Train, in the presence of your guildmaster, will advance you to the next level if he or she judges you worthy of it. If not, you will just work up a sweat.

At a guildmaster you can swap points from one stat to another. Use `train <stat>' at a guildmaster to see what the guildmaster will charge.

Example: train strength into perception
