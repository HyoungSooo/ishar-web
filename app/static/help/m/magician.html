Help on: magician

Arcane wisdom and manipulation of cosmic forces are the Magician's stock in trade. In a hostile world, his power to become invisible, see in the dark, and control other's minds allows him to come through many bad situations unscathed. His control over the destructive forces of nature enable him to be the most fearsome of foes. All this power comes at a price though; the required years of intense study does not allow for much physical conditioning, so the magician is the least physically durable class.

Skills: Spell casting - `help Spell List' for available spells.
