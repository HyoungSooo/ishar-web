Help on: multibox

The game currently allows you to log on as three characters at a time without any penalties. You are still asked to please not use character purely for storage or muling and to not share characters. It is understood that it is convenient to be able to log on as a high level mage or necro when no one is online, however that makes the already super loose nature and convenience of multiplay reach more ludicrous levels.
