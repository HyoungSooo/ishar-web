Help on: quitting

Have you been playing this game for years and don't find it fun any more? Do you yearn for the excitement of "how the game used to be", back when you still had something to learn about the game? Do you find that your performance at work or your grades in school are suffering, you are spending little or no time enjoying the company of real-life humans, and you have nothing to show for it except a nice kit and knowledge of how to kill obscure monsters? Have you tried to quit, but found that you couldn't stay away for more than a few hours or days?

You might be suffering from MUD addiction, a serious problem that affects veteran players. We can help you quit the habit and regain a life; just talk to any visible Eternal or higher.
