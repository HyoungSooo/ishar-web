Help on: flee

Syntax:flee
Level:any
Minimum:fle

If you bite off a little more than you can chew and find yourself overmatched in a fight, you may want to break away from combat and run away. Caution though, when you flee from a fight you are not looking where you are going, just running! So you might run right off a cliff or into an even more fearsome foe. Flee does not always work! You may fail your attempt to escape your foe, so if you flee manually, pay attention in case you fail and need to flee again. 'Wimpy' can help keep you out of trouble.

Fleeing from combat can only be attempted once per round, so be sure you are standing, or otherwise able to flee before attempting it. See help on Combat for more information.

See also: Combat, Wimpy
