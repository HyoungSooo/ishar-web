Help on: forgive

Syntax:forgive <person>
Level:any
Minimum:forgive

This will ask the system to forgive a fellow player for accidentally attacking you, stealing from you or other general aggressiveness. There is no list of who has wronged you or how they wronged you. Forgiving is all-absolving and immediate if the person needed it.

If you might have wronged someone, ask them to forgive you. This is crucial if you wish to avoid penalties imposed by the system, but it is completely up to the victim whether or not you deserve forgiveness.
