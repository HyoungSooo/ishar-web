Help on: rogue

Stealthy and agile, the Rogue can move unseen among his enemies and, through his knowledge of locks and traps, can gain access to even the most secure areas. His great agility makes him a difficult target and his ability to sneak up from the rear and stick sharp things in tender spots makes him a fearsome enemy. Rogues often find that they are much quicker to act than any other class. 

   Skills: Backstab, Steal, Sneak, Hide, Pick, Unpick, Feint
           Climb, Find Traps, Untrap, Kick, Evade, Trip
