Help on: reply

Syntax:reply <something>
Level:any
Minimum:rep

Like tell, reply provides communication with a single other person. Your reply is sent to the last person who used Tell, Ask, or Reply to communicate with you, even if you cannot currently see them.

You may unintentionally reply to the wrong person, if you are not careful.

See also: Tell, Ask, Quiet, Ignore, Communication, Invisibility
