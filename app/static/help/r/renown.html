Help on: renown

Through various acts of renown and valor in the world of Ishar, you will gain "Renown". Renown can be leveraged in a variety of ways; from upgrading your character to other, perhaps surprising, ways.

** Currently Renown is only gained through remorting and can only be used to upgrade your character. More to come.
