Help on: rooms

The basic building blocks of Ishar's world are rooms.

Rooms all have some features in common. All rooms have names, though not necessarily unique ones. Rooms have descriptions to tell you what to expect. Rooms usually have exits linking them together, some with doors.

Some rooms have special characteristics. Some are sound-proof, some are under a roof, some are lit, etc. Some exits will have locked doors, or traps, or ... you'll find some interesting rooms in Ishar.
