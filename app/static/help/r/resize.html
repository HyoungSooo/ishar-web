Help on: resize

Syntax:resize <object>
Level:any
Minimum:resiz

This will indicate to the Tinker shop that you wish to properly size a piece of armor to your body. This comes in handy when as a Human warrior you're wishing to try on the new dwarven chestplate you've just relieved from its previous owner. The Tinkers of course will charge for their services.

As for Tinkers in major towns: in Mareldja seek out Fumbles and in Cadain find Lumnor. There are rumors of wandering tinkers, but you've never been one for gossip ... have you?
