Help on: insecure

Syntax:insecure [<command>]
Level:any
Minimum:ins

While in Paranoid mode, your communication is normally not heard by anyone who is connected to Ishar with an unencrypted network connection.

If you do need to talk to someone on an unencrypted connection, you can either turn Paranoid mode off, communicate, and turn it back on, or just put the word "insecure" in front of that command.
