Help on: ishar hours

Syntax:time
Level:any
Minimum:tim

Show the current time of day, along with the date and lunar cycle.

In Isharian time there are 24 hours in a day, 30 days in a month, 7 days in a week, and 12 months in a year. 

   For comparison:      Ishar time   Real-time
                         1 hour     ~ 1 minute
                         1 day      ~24 minutes
                         1 week     ~ 3 hours
                         1 month    ~12 hours
                         1 year     ~ 6 days
