Help on: bound items

When an item is new, any player may use it. Whoever rents the item ten days after it was touched by a player will become the item's owner. Bound items are wearable only by the owner. They may still be looted from a corpse or stolen by a thief. Binding is permanent, a new object must be obtained if another person wishes to try the item.
