Help on: kick

Syntax:kick [<victim>]
Class:Warrior / Rogue
Level:1 / 11
Minimum:kic
Stats:Strength/Perception

Kick assumes the opponent is your target if you are in combat so you don't have to supply a <victim>. If you wish to initiate combat with a kick you have to supply the victim's name. Kick does damage but does not prevent an opponent fleeing or casting.

See also: Warrior, Combat, Protect
